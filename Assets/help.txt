Enter 'go' and a direction to move around the map. (go north)
Enter 'map' to see the map.
Enter 'get' and the item to get items on ground.
Enter 'drop' and the item to drop item.
Enter 'use' and the item to use item.
Enter 'talk [NPC NAME]' to talk to NPCs in the area.
Enter 'music on' or 'music off' to toggle music.
Enter 'quit game' to quit the game.
